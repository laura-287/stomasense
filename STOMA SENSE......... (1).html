Stoma Sense App Features

Smart Plant Care & Tracking

1. Welcome & Login

Splash screen, sign up/login via email or social, and support for Arabic, English, Chinese, and French.

2. Home Dashboard

Display popular plant categories with info, photos, and interactive options (like, comment, rate).

3. Agricultural Videos

Videos in multiple languages, comment box with interaction and rating options.

4. Upload Plant Images

Upload only plant photos, delete option, and automatic plant recognition.

5. Plant Growth Tracking

Track plant age, size, and care needs with visual growth comparison and multi-plant support.

6. Plant Search Page

Smart search bar with category filters and detailed plant information.

7. Categorized Plant Info

Organized by type, includes images, descriptions, and external links.

8. App Customization

Choose themes, languages, fonts, with support for offline use and dark mode.

9. User Profile

View and edit user information, profile photo, and logout option.

10. Sidebar Navigation

Quick navigation through a drawer-style menu.

11. Ads Integration

AdMob and StartApp integration with non-intrusive ad placement.

12. Instant Translation

Real-time content translation to support global users.
